Insurance covers

For comprehensive insurance coverage and treatment at Durdans, please follow the process below.

  • Contact our international care team and ask us about using your health insurance with Durdans.
  • Fill out the insurance form with your local doctor and send it to your coordinator, who will process your request through our insurance department.
  • We require pre-approval from insurance companies in out-patient and in-patient admission.

Checklist of Items to be Sent

  • Copy of your passport and Visa
  • A passport sized photo
  • A letter from the hospital you are currently receiving treatment from
  • Prior approval letters provided by your insurance company
